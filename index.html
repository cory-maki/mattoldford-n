Construction Expertise Meets Financial Intelligence

Matthew Oldford's career is built on a combination that rarely exists in a single person: deep hands-on construction skill and rigorous financial training. From coastal roofing projects to Scotiabank mortgage strategy to LIUNA foreman work, every stage of his career has added a new dimension to how he approaches development.

In 2018, he founded Matty's Renos — a renovation and design-build company that quickly earned a reputation for craftsmanship, transparency, and tangible return on investment. That company has since evolved into a full development operation, with multi-unit projects reshaping how Halifax grows.

Today Matt is completing a 17-unit building on Prince Albert Road and launching two purpose-built student-housing developments in Halifax's South End — each one a reflection of his commitment to quality supply in a city that needs it.
